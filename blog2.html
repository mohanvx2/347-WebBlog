Blog Entry #2:

Sep 24th, 2020

Readers,

In CS 347 we have seen a change in material over the past few weeks. This is primarily a change in the scope of the work we do, small-scale HTML is no longer a focus and we have moved on to things like Javascript. In addition we have moved forward with our semester-long projects and designed layouts in addition to getting requirements from our respective clientele. This is the most important part for me personally as I think it will give us a real look into how web-development is done on a large scale.

Another important piece of material we covered was related to the concept of Responsive Web Design. In this regard it was a good peer into usability concerns for not only our projects but for future endeavors as well. People are not always using the same devices or displays 24/7 and it is important that our resources are versatile enough for all users. As we move forward I hope we get into more material related to styling on a large scale as I believe that's where the majority of issues lie in terms of aesthetics for our projects.

Thank you all for reading!

- Varun
