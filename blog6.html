With the last half of the semester now under swing we have begun to speed up our material related to React and the Today App. This was done in an effort to get our second projects underway. Speaking of the second project, Xinzhe He and I have finally met up to discuss our plans and ideas.

This project will also be using influences and assets from our Databases course(CS 374) with Mayfield. That's all for this week but make sure to stay tuned in for updates as the project progresses!

Thank you all for reading!
