Readers,

As the semester runs to a close within the next week I thought it best to take a moment and reflect upon the things I've learned this semster. Starting this course off I had only back-end experience when it came to websites but as the course progressed I have been able to learn new concepts and implement them. The first project was a great learning experience for me personally and I thoroughly enjoyed the process of making it.

Related to the first project, Xinzhe and I have decided to switch our idea for the second project to a car purchasing service. This was done as a result of previous resources we had planned on using becoming defunct due to the CS departments policies. In terms of academics we breached the topic of security this week(pun intended :-) and discussed OAuth2. I found the material to be a nice foray into a more serious side of websites and the overlap with my CS 457(Information Security) class was very interesting to see. For the future Xinzhe and I will be continuing to work on our second project as the semester comes to a finish.

Thank you all for reading!
